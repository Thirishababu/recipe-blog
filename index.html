Crème Brûlée

Crème Brûlée is a creamy, silky custard topped with a layer of crunchy caramelized sugar. It's the perfect dessert to impress your guests!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Extra sugar for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C.
  2. Heat cream until warm but not boiling.
  3. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale.
  4. Slowly mix in the cream and vanilla.
  5. Pour into ramekins and bake in a water bath for 35-40 minutes.
  6. Chill for at least 2 hours.
  7. Sprinkle sugar and caramelize with a torch.
